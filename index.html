About Me

I’m a mixed media artist based in Arlington, TX specializing in sculpture and mosaic. I’ve always loved art; even from an early age I was always drawing. I spent a semester at the Rhode Island School of Design studying ceramics and went on to my receive my BFA from the University of North Texas emphasizing in sculpture, jewelry and fabric design. In addition to sculpture and mosaic, I paint, teach art, enjoy papercraft, and create miniature worlds.

Bragging Rights

TXFX Studios

5 years•

TXFX was a special effects make-up, and specialty prop shop. We were in business in the 90's, working on projects from low budget "B" horror flicks, to top rated video games, such as "DOOM". The company dissolved, and the primary artists moved to other projects, with Janimation and HIT Entertainment.

Projects I've Worked On

Yes, I really did.•

Doom, Id Software (video game) - "Revenant" sculpture and other steel and latex models for use as in-game monsters
Wishbone, TV show - model, animatronic and fabrication work
Savage Heroes, EA (Electronic Arts video game) - stop motion model creation
The Dummy, 1995 movie - prop team
